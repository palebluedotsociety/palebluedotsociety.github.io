The Pale Blue Dot Society

Goal
Nobody on Earth lacks the basic needs: food, water, clothing, shelter, and safety.

Why Now?
We are at a unique period of history where we have the technology and motivation to actually make this happen. We can do this without forcing our will upon those who don't want to help.

Non-Goals
Inequality of wealth is irrelevant to our goal. We don't care that some have more than others as long as those with the least have a good life.

Methods
Drive the cost of basic versions of these goods to zero, or as close to zero as possible. Then provide everyone on earth with enough universal basic income to buy these goods. Robotics and machine intelligence will play a key role. Imagine, for example, robotic farms that plant, weed, prune, water, and harvest crops, a plant at a time, at the optimal time for that particular shoot. Work that would be absurdly time consuming, not to mention annoying, for humans is ideal work for machines.

Who Will Pay For It?
We will. Our's will be the generation that provides this gift to all of humanity's descendants.

What Next?
We will debate ideas for how to achieve our goals. Some members may start companies to attempt to realize these goals.
